Timberborn is a city-building strategy game where you control a colony of intelligent beavers in a post-apocalyptic world. I hope to have more time in the near future to play it more consistently. In Timberborn, players must manage resources, build intricate water systems, and ensure their beavers' survival through seasonal droughts and floods. The game’s focus on water management adds depth, as players must plan for fluctuating water levels to keep crops irrigated and power essential structures.

The building mechanics are flexible, allowing for creative construction of dams, bridges, and more, while managing beaver needs like food and shelter. The game’s challenges vary with different biomes, making each playthrough unique.

What makes Timberborn fun is the balance of creativity and strategy. Players experiment with advanced systems while overcoming challenges like food shortages or flooding. With charming visuals and a relaxing soundtrack, the game provides a rewarding and enjoyable experience that keeps players engaged.
